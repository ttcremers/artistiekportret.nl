# Portfolio selection

This is the initial image set for the first editorial composition pass. The homepage should use a curated subset, not display every image.

- `https://zone.photos/images/phart/zonephotos-photoart-2.jpg`
  Likely homepage hero. Painterly historical portrait of a child. Immediate visual promise that this is not ordinary portrait photography.
- `https://zone.photos/images/phart/zonephotos-photoart-3.jpg`
  Strict classical portrait. Controlled and formal.
- `https://zone.photos/images/life-in-tone/zonephotos-life-in-tone-10.jpg`
  Panoramic sunset portrait. Also exists as a framed dibond print with a wooden frame and can support the portrait-as-physical-artwork proposition.
- `https://zone.photos/images/life-in-contrast/zonephotos-life-in-contrast-19.jpg`
  Strong grainy black-and-white portrait of a man.
- `https://zone.photos/images/life-in-contrast/zonephotos-life-in-contrast-20.jpg`
  Contrasty black-and-white portrait of a woman.
- `https://zone.photos/images/life-in-contrast/zonephotos-life-in-contrast-2.jpg`
  Film black-and-white portrait of a woman playing piano.
- `https://zone.photos/images/life-in-contrast/zonephotos-life-in-contrast-9.jpg`
  Landscape black-and-white portrait with a woman partly hidden behind a chair backrest, emphasizing the eyes.
- `https://zone.photos/images/life-in-contrast/zonephotos-life-in-contrast-16.jpg`
  Almost futuristic black-and-white portrait.
- `https://zone.photos/images/life-in-contrast/zonephotos-life-in-contrast-5.jpg`
  Slow, crafted 4x5 black-and-white portrait with a Venetian mask.
- `https://zone.photos/images/phart/zonephotos-photoart-4.jpg`
  Dutch Golden Age-inspired colour self-portrait. Suitable for the `Over mij` page.
- `https://zone.photos/images/phart/zonephotos-photoart-1.jpg`
  Conceptual self-portrait about the struggle with time. Better suited to autonomous work than the commercial homepage.
- `https://zone.photos/images/phart/zonephotos-photoart-8.jpg`
  Creative portrait emerging through a modern-art painting. Useful as the controlled authored rupture in the homepage sequence.
