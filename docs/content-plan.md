# Content plan

## Primary navigation

Keep the main navigation small and calm:

- **Portretten**
- **Autonoom werk**
- **Over mij**

Add a visible but quiet route to **Contact**. A separate **Werkwijze** page is likely useful and may appear in navigation or as a prominent supporting route from the homepage.

## Homepage

The homepage should act as a clear front door. It should quickly communicate:

- who Theodoor Thomas is
- that the work is artistic portrait photography in The Hague
- that the approach sits between a photoshoot and a painted portrait
- that the process is calm, personal and attentive
- that the result may become a finished physical artwork

Avoid overwhelming the visitor with too many galleries or choices. Start with one strong image and a small number of meaningful routes.

## Core pages

### Portretten

Show selected portrait work with enough context to suggest atmosphere, attention and variety without becoming a dense gallery wall.

### Werkwijze

Explain the process in reassuring language. Make clear that visitors do not need to know how to pose and that the session can develop at a calm pace.

### Autonoom werk

Provide space for personal projects and concept-driven work. This keeps the artistic identity visible without obscuring the commercial front door.

### Over mij

Introduce Theodoor Thomas in a transparent, human way. Explain the artistic name and the broader motivation behind the work.

### Contact

Make contact easy for people who prefer writing over calling. A simple form or email route is sufficient for the first version.

## Future landing pages

These pages may be useful later for both clarity and search visibility:

- `/portretten-voor-schrijvers-muzikanten-en-makers/`
- `/ik-sta-niet-graag-voor-een-camera/`
- `/portret-als-kunstwerk/`
- `/fine-art-nude/`

The fine-art nude section should have its own place within the site, but should not dominate the primary navigation or first impression.

## Language

Build the Dutch version first. Add an English layer later if there is a clear need, for example for international artists and expats in The Hague.
