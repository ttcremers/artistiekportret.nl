# Vision

## Purpose

`artistiekportret.nl` is the clear public front door for the artistic portrait work of **Theodoor Thomas** in The Hague.

The site should help a visitor understand, within a few moments, that this is not a standard photography service. The work sits between a photographic session and a painted portrait: accessible enough to begin with a camera, attentive enough to become a personal artwork.

## Core positioning

**Artistieke portretfotografie in Den Haag**

The offer is built around creativity, atmosphere, interpretation and calm attention. A portrait may begin as a session, but it can continue into a carefully selected image, an extensively developed work, and eventually a physical piece with print and frame.

## What the site should communicate

- A portrait can be more than a digital file.
- The process is unhurried and personal.
- The subject does not need to know how to pose or perform.
- The photographer brings a distinct visual voice.
- The final result may feel closer to an artwork than to a conventional photoshoot.

## Relationship to zone.photos

`zone.photos` may remain as a broader portfolio, archive and home for autonomous photography. `artistiekportret.nl` has a narrower task: attract people who may genuinely want to be portrayed.

## Brand name

Use **Theodoor Thomas** as the artistic name on the public site. The tone should remain transparent and human rather than theatrical.
