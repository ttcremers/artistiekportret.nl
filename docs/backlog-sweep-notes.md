- `http://zone.photos/artistiekportret.nl/styleguide/`
- `http://zone.photos/artistiekportret.nl/styleguide/style-guide.html`

The approved direction is a quiet contemporary gallery room: warm mineral background, near-black text, restrained yellow-gold accents, thin rules, square-edged images, editorial asymmetry and breathing room.

Avoid rounded card parades, nested panels, heavy shadows, autoplay, cursor-only behaviour and generic photography-site styling.
