# Audience

## Primary audience

The site is for people who want a portrait with character, atmosphere and attention rather than a generic photoshoot.

Natural audiences include:

- writers
- musicians
- visual artists and makers
- performers
- small entrepreneurs with a personal story
- people who feel a little different
- people who do not feel comfortable in front of a camera

## Emotional need

Many visitors may not be looking for a photographer because they enjoy being photographed. Some may actively dislike it. The site should make clear that they do not need to know how to pose, perform or manufacture confidence.

The process should feel calm, attentive and safe. Silence is allowed. Uncertainty is allowed. The aim is not to produce a polished version of the subject, but to arrive at an image that feels recognizably theirs.

## What we are not optimizing for

This is not a bulk headshot service, a generic studio package or a fast-turnaround content factory. The site should avoid language and design patterns that imply speed, volume or formulaic packages as the core value.

## Future audience-specific pages

- fine-art nude as a separate, quieter section within the site
